Glacier National Park, BC, Canada

{% comment %}
It was a cold and rainy day. The first few kilometres were soft dirt trail which later became rocky as we moved out of the treeline. There was a scramble at the top, which was quite slick from the rain. As we arrived to the summit, the rain suddenly stopped and the clouds parted for a passing moment, before we were shrouded in fog again. The view was stunning and unfortunately my phone camera could not do it justice.
